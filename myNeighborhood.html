Asakusa Tokyo

Welcome to Asakusa!!

Asakusa is located in Tokyo, Japan.

Asakusa is a place you can feel traditional Japanese culture while you are in Tokyo.

There are traditional temples and shrines that you can visit, kimonos(traditional clothes) that you can try on, and especially there are Japanese traditional delicious food!

There are many great restaurants in Asakusa.
